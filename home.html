About

My Brother’s Keeper is a registered non-profit organization operating from Soweto with a focus on bridging the gap between private schools and public schools in South Africa by getting volunteers from private schools and people in business and other circles to share knowledge and skills so that the rest of the country can prosper and help heals the scares of the past and create a nation where we all start seeing each other as a single people united beyond skin and language.
